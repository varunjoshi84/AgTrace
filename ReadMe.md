# AgTrace – Full-Stack Supply Chain Transparency Platform

AgTrace is a modern, production-ready web application that brings complete visibility to agricultural supply chains – from farm to customer – using QR codes and role-based tracking.

Farmers, transporters, market officers, packaging centers, and retailers all update the same digital record as produce moves through the supply chain. Customers simply scan a QR code on the product to see the full verified journey: who grew it, when it was harvested, how it was transported (with GPS route & temperature history), its quality grade, packaging details, and arrival at the store.
